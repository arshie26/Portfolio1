Restaurant Website Relaunch Under Pressure

Working at an agency, I managed the transformation of a website from a clunky storefront to a gleaming customer experience without impacting business or losing the brand experience.

The client was very happy with the redesign and felt much more confident about advertising their business moving forward.

THE CHALLENGE

Brand and Website Redesign

Three days before a soft launch, The client introduced new, critical requirements. The existing website in development was suddenly no longer sufficient. The launch timeline could not move.

Update Domain

The existing domain was no longer acceptable.

No Funnel

Embedding a third-party reservation system

Platform Issues

Platform limitations, paywalls and credential problems conflicted with requirements.

Tight Timeline

Escalating pressure meant tense communications and negotiations.

Antiquated

A major redesign was required within about one week.

Team Dynamics

Team dynamics required me to pick up slack and get the job done.

THE APPROACH

My Role: Project Manager

Deliver a brand-aligned, functional restaurant website that:

Was easy to navigate and fun to use

Launched on time — without escalation
